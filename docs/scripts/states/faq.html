Frequently Asked Questions

How can we help?

If you would like to cancel your booking Mystery Odyssey charges a cancellation fee of GBP 20 per traveller. The fee does NOT include the fees charged by the airline, hotel, car rental or activity provider. These fees will be passed on to you by Mystery Odyssey.

You will get all your money back as long as your cancellation is not too close to the check-in period of 7 days.

If you would like to change your holiday booking, Mystety Odyssey charges a fee of GBP 10 to make the changes. The fee does NOT include the airline chnage fee for each ticket or the hotel change fee. These fees will be passed on to you by Mystery Odyssey.

If you request to change your activity preference within 24 hours of your confirmed booking this can happen through Mystery Odysseys' online booking system. If change is required after more than 24 hours of your confirmed booking this can be done upon arrival at the destionation.

For your flight check-in you will need your confirmation code from your holiday booking.
