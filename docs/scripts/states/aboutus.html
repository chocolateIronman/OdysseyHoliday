Our Company

The company name and group name is under Mystery Odyssey

We thought that this name was appropriate for the service that our company offers, as it provides a clear insight as to what we are about as a company.

The company is made up of five members, each with different skills to help the business thrive!

What is it and how does our service work?

We offer a unique service of finding you the perfect holiday for you, using a criteria and budget that you provide us with, to then send you off on a mystery holiday experience. The criteria may be things like hot or cold location, chilled or high octane activities.

What is different about what we offer is that with most travel firms you know what you are doing and where you are going, as you have decided. But we offer our service to decide all this for you, like the location etc. So that is what sets us apart from our competitors, as they do not offer a service such as this.

The service we offer is Mystery travel experiences for customers, who want to live an exciting lifestyle.
